# EDEN roadmap

Everything below is future work. None is required for or represented as part of v0.1.

## v0.2 — ecological relationships

- Predators and bounded attack/flee behavior.
- Scavenging and decomposer nutrient loops.
- Sexual reproduction with compatible mate selection.
- Richer local sensing and inherited signal interpretation.
- Multi-seed ecology calibration and on-Pi performance profiles.

## v0.3 — communication and niches

- Learned signal channels without language models.
- Nesting, shelter, and lightweight construction.
- More explicit ecological niches and food types.
- Experiment comparison tools and lineage replay.
- Optional synthesized ambient audio and accessible sound controls.

## v0.4 — physical artifact integration

- Real-room temperature, light, or humidity sensors.
- Rotary controls and physical intervention buttons.
- E-ink or secondary status display.
- Read-only local-network observer with no cloud dependency.
- Multiple isolated worlds for controlled experiments.

## Long-range research directions

- Tribes and cultural inheritance only after ecological performance remains bounded.
- More expressive but still tiny recurrent controllers.
- Richer physics that remain compatible with Raspberry Pi 5.
- Reproducible multi-world evolutionary studies.

Civilization, politics, complex economics, realistic fluids, photorealism, online multiplayer, cloud services, and LLM agents are intentionally outside the near-term scope.
